Shipping And Refunds

Easy Refunds and Returns

    Easy Return:

    We have a 100% Buyer protection policy wherein you can return the product for any of the below reasons:

    1. Received a wrong size to what was ordered
    2. Received the wrong product
    3. Received a damaged product
    4. Package missing product components
    Return Process:

    Just email us your complaint with a picture of the received product within 5 days of receipt of the product.

    Our customer care will reach out to solve your particular case.

    Refund:

    If you wish, we can offer you a redeemable voucher worth the refund amount for future purchases. Else we can also refund the entire refund amount back to your bank account without any deductions along.
